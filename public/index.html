Dining

Two restaurants.
One obsession with the Sonoran.

From wood-fired mesquite plates to a rooftop bar with the best sunset in Tucson, every menu is built around what's growing — and ranching — within 200 miles.

Coyote & Sage
Signature · Farm-to-Table

Chef Marisol Vega's love letter to the Sonoran Desert: heritage corn, mesquite-grilled meats, and a tasting menu that changes with what the local farms bring in. Reservations strongly recommended.

Dinner · Tue – Sun · 5:30 – 10 PM
Brunch · Sat & Sun · 9 AM – 1 PM
Mirador Rooftop
Cocktails · Small Plates

Our open-air rooftop lounge: prickly-pear margaritas, mezcal flights, and a small-plates menu designed to share. The best perch in town for the sunset.

Bar · Wed – Sun · 4 PM – 12 AM
Kitchen · Wed – Sun · 4 – 10 PM
Mesa Café
All-Day · Casual

Our breakfast and lunch room, off the lobby. Locally roasted Yellow Brick coffee, house-baked pastries, breakfast burritos, and a daily green-chile stew.

Daily · 6:30 AM – 3 PM
